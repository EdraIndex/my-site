Additional disclosures for edraindex.com

Specific to the EDRA™ website — supplements, and does not replace, the policy above

These additional disclosures describe technologies and processing that are specific to the edraindex.com website. They supplement the OutsourcingHubIndia Privacy Policy above; where the two address the same topic, both apply.

A1. Website analytics & visitor identification

To understand how our website is used and to identify organisations that may be interested in our services, edraindex.com uses the following third-party technologies. These may set cookies and/or process your IP address, device, and browsing activity. Each provider acts as an independent processor or controller of the data it receives, under its own privacy policy.

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — measures website traffic and usage so we can improve the site. Processed by Google.
  • Google Ads — conversion measurement and remarketing tags that help us understand which marketing activity leads to enquiries. Processed by Google.
  • Microsoft Clarity — aggregated session analytics, heatmaps, and (where enabled) session replay to diagnose usability issues. Processed by Microsoft.
  • RB2B — identifies, where possible, the organisation or professional associated with a website visit, for business-to-business outreach.
  • Leadfeeder / Dealfront — identifies the company associated with a visit using IP and firmographic data, for business-to-business sales.
  • Lusha — website-visitor identification and business-contact enrichment, for business-to-business sales.

You can control or refuse cookies through your browser settings, opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics opt-out add-on, and manage Google ad personalisation in your Google Ad Settings. To ask us to stop processing data associated with you through these tools, contact us using the details below.

A2. Email open & click tracking

When we send you email — including outreach, updates, and replies to your enquiries — we may use tracking technologies such as a small embedded image ("pixel") and uniquely tagged links. These allow us to see whether and when an email was opened, how many times, the approximate device or location, and whether you clicked a link within it.

We use this information to understand how our communications are received, gauge interest, time our follow-ups, and improve the relevance of what we send. For recipients in the EU/UK, our lawful basis for this is our legitimate interest in communicating effectively with current and prospective clients; we balance this against your privacy and limit tracking to what is necessary for that purpose.

You can opt out at any time: configure your email client to block remote images (which disables pixel tracking), avoid clicking tracked links, or email us at hello@edraindex.com to ask us to stop tracking your messages or to be removed from our mailing list. Email-tracking data is retained only as long as needed for the purposes above and is not sold or shared except with the service providers that help us deliver and analyse our email.

A3. Forms & enquiries

When you submit a form on edraindex.com — such as the "Request an Assessment" form — we collect the details you provide (for example your name, work email, company, portfolio type, approximate number of assets, primary objective, and any message). We use a third-party email delivery provider (Resend) to send this information to our team so we can respond to your enquiry, and we may retain it to follow up and for our records. We do not use these details for unrelated purposes or sell them.
