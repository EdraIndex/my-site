EDRATM · Australia

ESG data readiness for Australian portfolios

EDRATM is the diagnostic layer that tests whether an Australian portfolio's ESG data can withstand AASB S2, NGER, CER, and investor scrutiny — before the reporting cycle begins.

AASB S2 · ASRS NGER CER · Safeguard NABERS GRESB CDP
Readiness Scan

Known vs unknown

Five diagnostic questions we ask every Australian portfolio before scoring, certification, or AASB S2 disclosure.

01Do you have complete, usable energy data across your portfolio?Across owned, leased, and operator-managed assets.
02Can you separate landlord, tenant, operator, and asset-level boundaries?The boundary issue that breaks most US disclosures.
03Do your ESG datasets survive audit, benchmarking, and certification scrutiny?AASB S2, NGER, CER, GRESB, and big-four assurance review.
04Can your finance, ESG, and asset teams trust the same data source?One number, defensible across CFO, sustainability, and operations.
05Do you know what you do not know before the reporting cycle begins?Gap discovery is the cheapest moment in the cycle.
