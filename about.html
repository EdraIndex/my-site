About

The bridge to sustainability data

Sustainability data is where the world is moving — and someone has to account for it. EDRATM brings accounting-grade discipline to that data, so portfolios can measure, trust, and report it with the same confidence as their financials.

EDRATM

EDRA is the data-readiness layer for sustainability reporting. It scores portfolio data, surfaces the gaps that suppress performance, and structures the dataset for the frameworks it must satisfy — turning raw ESG inputs into decision-grade, auditable evidence. Score it, fix it, report it.

Outsourcing Hub India

EDRA is built and operated by Outsourcing Hub India — a 600-person, technology-driven data and operations firm. Our team is GRESB-certified, with deep, hands-on knowledge of sustainability data: its sources, its gaps, and the frameworks it must meet. Sustainability data is, quite literally, what the world is moving to — and accounting for it is our core.
