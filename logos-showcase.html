Usage Notes

Geometric Mark

  • Use as a brand poster, social cover, pitch deck header, or proposal cover
  • The isometric cubes represent EDRA's 3-plane scoring architecture (A / B / C)
  • Wireframes = target state. Solid = current state. The visual embodies EDRA's core concept
  • Best at full size (900×900 or larger) — detail is lost at small sizes

Logo V1 — Mark + Wordmark

  • Primary logo for website nav, presentations, email signatures
  • The scoring-bar mark can be used standalone as a favicon
  • Minimum width: 160px. Below this, omit the tagline
  • Dark bg: warm white wordmark. Light bg: ink (#0D1F14) wordmark. Gold mark works on both

Logo V2 — Typographic

  • Masthead, cover page, large-format brand statement
  • The gold "A" creates a visual anchor — preserve the weight contrast
  • Minimum width: 200px to maintain legibility of ruled lines
  • Tagline is optional below 140px — omit cleanly
