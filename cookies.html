Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and settings.

2. Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Authentication
  • Security
  • Preferences
  • Analytics
  • Marketing

3. Types of cookies we use

  • Analytics Cookies: Understand site interaction.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember user choices like language preferences.
  • Marketing Cookies: These are used to show you advertisements that are relevant to your interests.
