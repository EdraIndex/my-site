The Problem

Why EDRATM exists

Most portfolios submit ESG data without knowing whether it meets certification thresholds. EDRATM changes that.

Without EDRATM

  • Portfolios submit without knowing whether data meets threshold logic
  • Coverage gaps block performance scoring
  • Missing structural fields break asset eligibility
  • Billing discontinuity corrupts consumption history
  • Governance weaknesses make data non-defensible

With EDRATM

  • Every asset is scored before reporting starts
  • Every blocker is tagged with a reason code
  • Remediation is prioritized by certification impact
  • Outputs are reviewable, auditable, and portfolio-wide
  • Teams know exactly what to fix next

Built inside real estate operations, not outside them.

EDRATM comes from years of working with portfolio data, financial operations, and reporting workflows across real estate portfolios in the US, Canada, and UK.
