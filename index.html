About EDRATM

The ESG certification blind spot nobody talks about.

  • 2,000+ entities report to GRESB covering $8.8 trillion in real estate assets
  • 150+ institutional investors worldwide use GRESB benchmarks to allocate capital
  • Most portfolios submit without knowing if their data meets coverage, completeness, or continuity thresholds
  • Consultants advise on strategy — nobody scans the data first

EDRATM was built to solve this at the source. Score every asset across three planes. Flag every gap with a codified reason code. Close every gap with tech and humans in the loop.
