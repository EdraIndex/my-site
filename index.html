Methodology

The rules EDRATM will not break

01
Null is never treated as zeroMissing data is flagged, never silently scored.
02
Coverage is tested before performanceNo performance score until the data qualifies.
03
Continuity is judged at asset levelBilling gaps are caught per asset, not in aggregate.
04
Inference is avoided where evidence is missingWe don't estimate what can't be substantiated.
05
Threshold logic is explicit, not impliedEvery gate is documented and reproducible.
06
Every correction carries an audit trailOriginal, corrected, rationale, approver, timestamp.
